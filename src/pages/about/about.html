Description of the app

Luggage sharing app is designed by the Prakash and Sunil. This app is helpful at the airport to share passangers their luggage. The main moto for developing this app is that saving the money of passengers which they paid to airways as their extra charges for the overweight. The charges the companies applying for the overweight are not the regular charges.
